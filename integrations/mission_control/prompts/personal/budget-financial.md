## Workspace File Organization — Budget / Financial Planning

Organize channel workspace files to manage personal or small business finances:

- **budget.md** — Monthly budget by category
  - Format: table with Category, Planned, Actual, Variance columns
  - Categories: housing, food/groceries, transportation, utilities, insurance, entertainment, savings, debt payments, etc.
  - Track month-to-date spending against planned amounts
  - Highlight overspending with notes on cause
  - Running totals: total income, total expenses, net savings

- **transactions.md** — Running transaction log
  - Format: `- YYYY-MM-DD | $amount | Category | Description`
  - Append new entries at the top (most recent first)
  - Group by month with monthly subtotals
  - Flag irregular or unexpected transactions for review
  - Reconciliation notes against bank statements

- **goals.md** — Financial goals and savings targets
  - Format: `### Goal Name` with target amount, current amount, deadline, progress bar
  - Goal types: emergency fund, debt payoff, investment, large purchase, retirement
  - Monthly contribution targets per goal
  - Milestone celebrations and adjustment notes

- **forecasts.md** — Projections and scenario planning
  - 3-6 month expense projections based on trends
  - Scenario planning: best case, expected, worst case
  - Upcoming known expenses (insurance renewals, annual fees, holidays)
  - Income projections and variable income tracking
  - Net worth trend tracking

- **tasks.md** — Kanban board for financial action items (Mission Control compatible)

- **status.md** — Financial health overview (Mission Control compatible)

- **timeline.md** — Reverse-chronological activity log (Mission Control compatible)
  - Auto-captures financial events: goals reached, budget reviews completed, large transactions, account changes
  - Entries: `- HH:MM — Event description` grouped under `## YYYY-MM-DD` date headers
  - Append new entries at top of current day's section
  - Use `append_timeline_event` tool to log events (also auto-logged by `move_task_card`, status changes, etc.)

- **notes.md** — Financial notes and scratch space
  - Research on investment options or accounts
  - Tax preparation notes and deduction tracking
  - Insurance policy details and renewal dates
  - Subscription audit and cancellation candidates

Create files as needed — not all files are required from the start. Archive past monthly budgets and quarterly forecasts to the archive/ folder.

### tasks.md — Kanban Format

Use a markdown kanban board with these columns:

```markdown
## Backlog

### Research high-yield savings accounts
- **id**: mc-a1b2c3
- **priority**: medium
- **tags**: savings, research

### Review and cancel unused subscriptions
- **id**: mc-p6q7r8
- **priority**: low
- **tags**: expenses, optimization

## In Progress

### Set up automatic transfers to investment account
- **id**: mc-d4e5f6
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: investing, automation
- **started**: 2026-03-28

### Track March expenses against budget
- **id**: mc-s9t0u1
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: budget, monthly-review
- **started**: 2026-03-25

## Review

### Compare car insurance renewal quotes
- **id**: mc-g7h8i9
- **priority**: medium
- **tags**: insurance, renewal
- **started**: 2026-03-20

## Done

### File Q1 estimated tax payment
- **id**: mc-j0k1l2
- **priority**: critical
- **tags**: taxes, deadline
- **completed**: 2026-03-15

### Consolidate old 401k into current account
- **id**: mc-v2w3x4
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: retirement, accounts
- **completed**: 2026-03-10
```

Each card follows the Mission Control card format:
- **id** (required): Unique `mc-XXXXXX` identifier (auto-generated by `create_task_card`)
- **priority**: `low`, `medium`, `high`, or `critical`
- **tags**: Comma-separated labels (e.g. budget, savings, investing, taxes, insurance, debt)
- **started** / **completed**: ISO date timestamps (auto-set by `move_task_card`)

Use `create_task_card` and `move_task_card` tools for all task management — tasks.md is a read-only rendering from the database and must not be edited directly.

### status.md — Financial Health Format

```markdown
- **phase**: Monthly Tracking — March 2026
- **health**: yellow
- **updated**: 2026-03-28
- **owner**: Self

## Current Focus
- March budget tracking — 3 days remaining
- Setting up automatic investment transfers
- Car insurance renewal decision by April 5

## Blockers
- Awaiting 401k rollover confirmation (submitted March 10)

## Financial Snapshot

| Category | Planned | Actual | Variance |
|----------|---------|--------|----------|
| Income | $6,200 | $6,200 | -- |
| Housing | $1,800 | $1,800 | -- |
| Food | $600 | $720 | -$120 |
| Transport | $300 | $250 | +$50 |
| Savings | $1,000 | $1,000 | -- |
| Other | $1,500 | $1,380 | +$120 |

## Goal Progress
- Emergency fund: $8,500 / $10,000 (85%)
- Student loan payoff: $12,200 remaining (was $18,000)
- Vacation fund: $600 / $2,000 (30%)

## Recent Milestones
- 2026-03-15: Q1 estimated tax filed on time
- 2026-03-10: Old 401k rollover initiated — $34,500
- 2026-03-01: February budget closed — $180 under total budget
```

Health values: `green` (on budget, goals on track), `yellow` (overspending in categories or behind on goal pace), `red` (significant overspending, missed payments, or emergency fund depleted).

### timeline.md — Activity Log

Reverse-chronological event stream capturing financial activity. New entries go at the top of the current day's section.

```markdown
## 2026-03-28

- 20:00 — Budget update: food category at $720 vs $600 planned — dining out overspend
- 15:00 — Card mc-d4e5f6 moved to **In Progress** (was: Backlog) — "Set up automatic investment transfers"
- 12:00 — Transaction logged: $45 groceries (Trader Joe's)
- 09:00 — Emergency fund contribution: $500 — new balance $8,500 (85% of goal)

## 2026-03-27

- 18:00 — Car insurance quotes received: 3 options ranging $140-$185/month
- 16:00 — Card mc-g7h8i9 moved to **Review** (was: In Progress) — "Compare insurance quotes"
- 14:00 — Subscription audit: identified $35/month in unused services
- 10:00 — Card mc-j0k1l2 moved to **Done** (was: In Progress) — "File Q1 estimated tax"
```

Events are auto-logged by `move_task_card` and status changes. Use `append_timeline_event` to manually log notable events (goal milestones, budget reviews, large transactions, account changes, tax deadlines). Heartbeats can auto-append monthly financial summaries for passive monitoring.

### plans.md — Structured Execution Plans (Read-Only Rendering)

Plans are stored in the MC database. `plans.md` is auto-generated after every state change — never edit it directly. Use `draft_plan` to create plans, and `update_plan_step`/`update_plan_status` for mutations. After approval, the plan executor automatically sequences step execution. Pull the planning skill for the full protocol.
