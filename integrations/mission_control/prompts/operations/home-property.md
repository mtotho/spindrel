## Workspace File Organization — Home / Property Management

Organize channel workspace files to manage home or property operations:

- **properties.md** — Property details and key information
  - Format: `### Property Name / Address` with key details
  - Square footage, lot size, year built, property type
  - Insurance policy details: provider, policy number, coverage, renewal date
  - Warranty information: appliances, roof, HVAC, with expiration dates
  - Utility account numbers and provider contacts
  - HOA details and fee schedule (if applicable)

- **maintenance.md** — Maintenance schedule and checklists
  - Seasonal maintenance checklists (spring, summer, fall, winter)
  - Recurring tasks with frequency: monthly, quarterly, annually
  - Format: `### Season / Category` with checklist items and last-completed dates
  - Equipment service records (HVAC filter changes, water heater flush, etc.)
  - Age tracking for major systems: roof, HVAC, water heater, appliances

- **contractors.md** — Service provider contacts and history
  - Format: `### Contractor Name` with specialty, phone, email, license number
  - Rating (1-5) based on past experience
  - Past work history: date, description, cost, quality notes
  - Pricing references for common jobs
  - Preferred vs backup providers per specialty (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, landscaping, etc.)

- **expenses.md** — Maintenance and improvement expense tracking
  - Format: `- YYYY-MM-DD | $amount | Category | Description | Contractor`
  - Categories: repair, maintenance, improvement, utility, insurance, tax
  - Monthly and annual totals
  - Budget tracking: planned vs actual by category
  - Capital improvement log for tax purposes

- **tasks.md** — Kanban board for property tasks (Mission Control compatible)

- **status.md** — Property health overview (Mission Control compatible)

- **timeline.md** — Reverse-chronological activity log (Mission Control compatible)
  - Auto-captures property events: maintenance completed, contractor visits, inspections, repairs, expense milestones
  - Entries: `- HH:MM — Event description` grouped under `## YYYY-MM-DD` date headers
  - Append new entries at top of current day's section
  - Use `append_timeline_event` tool to log events (also auto-logged by `move_task_card`, status changes, etc.)

- **notes.md** — Property notes, improvement ideas, and reference
  - Home improvement project ideas with rough cost estimates
  - Neighbor and HOA contact information
  - Permit requirements and zoning notes
  - Appliance model numbers and manual links
  - Paint colors, materials, and finish specifications by room

Create files as needed — not all files are required from the start. Archive completed improvement projects and past annual expense summaries to the archive/ folder.

### tasks.md — Kanban Format

Use a markdown kanban board with these columns:

```markdown
## Backlog

### Get quotes for deck staining
- **id**: mc-a1b2c3
- **priority**: medium
- **tags**: exterior, improvement

### Research smart thermostat options
- **id**: mc-p6q7r8
- **priority**: low
- **tags**: hvac, upgrade, energy

## Scheduled

### Schedule annual HVAC inspection
- **id**: mc-d4e5f6
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: hvac, maintenance, annual
- **started**: 2026-03-28

### Spring gutter cleaning
- **id**: mc-s9t0u1
- **priority**: medium
- **tags**: exterior, seasonal, spring
- **started**: 2026-03-27

## In Progress

### Fix leaking kitchen faucet
- **id**: mc-g7h8i9
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: plumbing, repair
- **started**: 2026-03-25

## Done

### Replace smoke detector batteries
- **id**: mc-j0k1l2
- **priority**: critical
- **tags**: safety, maintenance
- **completed**: 2026-03-20

### Winterize irrigation system
- **id**: mc-v2w3x4
- **priority**: high
- **tags**: landscaping, seasonal, winter
- **completed**: 2026-03-01
```

Each card follows the Mission Control card format:
- **id** (required): Unique `mc-XXXXXX` identifier (auto-generated by `create_task_card`)
- **priority**: `low`, `medium`, `high`, or `critical`
- **tags**: Comma-separated labels (e.g. plumbing, hvac, electrical, exterior, seasonal, repair, improvement)
- **started** / **completed**: ISO date timestamps (auto-set by `move_task_card`)

Use `create_task_card` and `move_task_card` tools for all task management — tasks.md is a read-only rendering from the database and must not be edited directly.

### status.md — Property Status Format

```markdown
- **phase**: Active Management — Spring Maintenance Season
- **health**: yellow
- **updated**: 2026-03-28
- **owner**: Homeowner

## Current Focus
- Kitchen faucet leak repair — plumber scheduled March 30
- Spring maintenance checklist — 4 of 9 items complete
- HVAC annual inspection due by April 15

## Blockers
- Waiting on plumber availability for faucet repair

## Property Health

| System | Age | Condition | Next Service |
|--------|-----|-----------|--------------|
| HVAC | 6 years | Good | April 2026 (annual) |
| Roof | 12 years | Fair | Inspect fall 2026 |
| Water Heater | 8 years | Good | Flush June 2026 |
| Appliances | Various | Good | -- |
| Exterior Paint | 4 years | Good | Repaint ~2028 |

## Expenses (2026 YTD)
- Maintenance: $1,240
- Repairs: $380
- Improvements: $0
- Total: $1,620 (budget: $6,000/year)

## Recent Milestones
- 2026-03-20: Smoke detectors updated — all 8 units tested
```

Health values: `green` (all systems operational, maintenance current), `yellow` (overdue maintenance or minor repair needed), `red` (urgent repair, safety issue, or major system failure).

### timeline.md — Activity Log

Reverse-chronological event stream capturing property activity. New entries go at the top of the current day's section.

```markdown
## 2026-03-28

- 16:00 — Plumber scheduled for March 30 — Mike's Plumbing, est. $150-$250
- 14:00 — Card mc-d4e5f6 moved to **Scheduled** (was: Backlog) — "Annual HVAC inspection"
- 11:00 — Spring checklist: cleaned dryer vent (item 5 of 9)
- 09:30 — Status health changed: green → yellow — kitchen faucet leak worsening

## 2026-03-27

- 17:00 — Expense logged: $85 — gutter cleaning supplies (Home Depot)
- 15:00 — Card mc-s9t0u1 moved to **Scheduled** (was: Backlog) — "Spring gutter cleaning"
- 13:00 — Card mc-j0k1l2 moved to **Done** (was: In Progress) — "Replace smoke detector batteries"
- 10:00 — Contractor note: got deck staining quote from Tom's Painting — $1,200
```

Events are auto-logged by `move_task_card` and status changes. Use `append_timeline_event` to manually log notable events (contractor visits, repairs completed, expenses logged, inspections, seasonal maintenance milestones). Heartbeats can auto-append weekly property summaries for passive monitoring.

### plans.md — Structured Execution Plans (Read-Only Rendering)

Plans are stored in the MC database. `plans.md` is auto-generated after every state change — never edit it directly. Use `draft_plan` to create plans, and `update_plan_step`/`update_plan_status` for mutations. After approval, the plan executor automatically sequences step execution. Pull the planning skill for the full protocol.
