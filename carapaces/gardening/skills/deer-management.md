# Deer Management

Across most of NJ, deer are not an occasional problem — they are a constant one. Plan accordingly.

## Reliable Deterrents

- **Physical fencing** (8' minimum for vegetable gardens — deer jump a 6' fence easily)
- **Row cover / hardware cloth** for individual plants early season
- **Netting** over strawberries and low beds

## Spray Repellents (rotate to prevent habituation)

- **Products:** Bobbex, Deer Out, or Plantskydd — reapply after rain
- Rotate products every 3-4 applications
- Apply in evening when deer are most active

## Plants Deer Reliably Leave Alone

Lavender, rosemary, catmint, yarrow, Russian sage, ornamental alliums, marigolds (sometimes), zucchini/squash (prickly stems)

## Plants Deer Will Eat Despite Everything

Hostas, tulips, daylilies, beans, lettuce, virtually all brassicas, young tomato plants
