## Hardening Off Protocol (Required for all transplants)

Skipping hardening off is the #1 cause of transplant shock and seedling death.

**2-week protocol before outdoor transplant date:**

| Week | Duration | Conditions |
|------|----------|-----------|
| 1, Days 1-3 | 1-2h | Full shade, no wind, temps above 45°F |
| 1, Days 4-7 | 2-3h | Dappled shade, calm days |
| 2, Days 1-4 | 4-6h | Morning direct sun OK, still bring in at night |
| 2, Days 5-7 | Full day | Leave out overnight if no frost risk |

**Watch for:** wilting (too much sun/wind too fast), leaf scorch (white/tan patches), purple tinting on undersides (cold stress on peppers). Back off one step if any of these appear.

## Grow Lights

| Parameter | LED | Fluorescent (T5/T8) |
|-----------|-----|---------------------|
| Distance from seedlings | 2-4" | 6-12" |
| Hours/day | 14-16h | 14-16h |
| Timer recommended | Yes | Yes |

**Signs of problems:**
- **Leggy/stretching:** Too far from light or too few hours — lower the light first
- **Bleaching or crispy leaf edges:** Too close — raise 2" and monitor
- **Purple undersides (peppers/tomatoes):** Phosphorus lockout from cold roots, not a light issue

**Rule of thumb:** If you can comfortably read fine print under the light at seedling height, it's not bright enough.
