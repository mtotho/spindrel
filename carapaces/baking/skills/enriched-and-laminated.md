# Enriched & Laminated Doughs

## Brioche

| Ingredient | Baker's % |
|-----------|-----------|
| Bread flour | 100% |
| Butter (cold, cubed) | 50-80% |
| Eggs | 50% |
| Sugar | 5-10% |
| Salt | 1.5% |
| Instant yeast | 2% |
| Milk | 10% |

**Method:** Develop gluten fully before adding butter. Add butter in small pieces over 15-20 min. Dough should be shiny and pull clean from bowl. 2-3h bulk, overnight cold proof.

---

## Challah

| Ingredient | Baker's % |
|-----------|-----------|
| Bread flour | 100% |
| Water | 10% |
| Eggs | 35% |
| Oil | 10% |
| Sugar | 15% |
| Salt | 1.5% |
| Instant yeast | 2% |

Braid after 1.5h bulk. Proof 1h until puffy. Egg wash (1 egg + 1 tbsp water), bake 350°F for 30-35 min.

## Croissants

**Dough (détrempe):**

| Ingredient | Baker's % |
|-----------|-----------|
| AP or T55 flour | 100% |
| Milk | 55% |
| Sugar | 10% |
| Salt | 2% |
| Butter | 10% |
| Instant yeast | 2% |

**Butter block (beurrage):** 250-300g European-style butter (84%+ fat), cold but pliable (59-63°F ideal)

**Lamination process:**
1. Make dough, chill overnight
2. Encase butter block in dough (book fold), roll out
3. **Letter fold (tour simple)** — 3 folds total, 30-60 min fridge rest between each
4. Final roll: 5mm thick
5. Cut: 8cm wide base × 20cm tall triangle
6. Roll from base to tip, stretch tip slightly
7. Proof 2-2.5h at 75-78°F (should jiggle visibly when pan is shaken)
8. Egg wash (yolk + cream), bake 375°F for 15-18 min

**Butter temp is critical:** Too cold = shatters and layers tear. Too warm = absorbs into dough. Touch test: butter should bend without cracking.

---

## Puff Pastry

- Same butter block, 6 letter folds, 20 min rest between each
- Never add yeast
- Bake 400-425°F from frozen for best lift
